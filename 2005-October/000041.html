Roland,

I would avoid the integration of CC into this proposal as CC is under  
heavy pressure and seems very contraproductive to mix again crystal  
clear technology research/development and a highly risky area. I do  
not suggest the NoE as it does not offer enough support for  
development. A big IP would be ideal with some large partners.
