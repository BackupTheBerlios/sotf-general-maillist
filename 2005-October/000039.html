Hi -

The strategic objective "Networked Audio Visual Systems and Home  
Platforms" will have an additional call for proposals in the IST  
programme: they are looking for 2-3 really big projects and some  
networks for excellence. This would be an excellent chance to submit a  
follow-up project for StreamOnTheFly, extending it with the scope for  
video, or integrating EPG collaborative filtering, or offering a CC  
publishing suite.

I had tried a proposal once in the objective "Networked Audio Visual  
Systems and Home Platforms", in 2003, but failed. The proposal was  
focussing on content clearing, see  
http://www.pvl.at/research/fp6/pvldocument_view
They did not like, that we mix policy making and establishing new  
technologies.

If you are interested to join a consortium, please indicate, which  
parts of the proposal you would be able to re-phrase.

Maybe somebody is able to go to Brussels on Oct 18th.

-Roland
