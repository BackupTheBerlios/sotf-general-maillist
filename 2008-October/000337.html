hi everyone,

i'm invited to speak about online archives in the context of free radios 
and community radios at http://www.nordicradioconference2008.com in 
turku (finland) next week. in this regard i'll talk about our (orange's) 
experiences in working with online archives in general and sotf in 
particular. the sotf project seems fallen into a pretty deep sleep, 
maybe it can be kissed awake at this opportunity.

running at this time. i will also say a few words about the video node, 
maybe show http://media.xaok.org/ as an example. but since the 
conference is focused to radio interests the audio version will be more 
important.
