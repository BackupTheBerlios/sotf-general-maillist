hello everyone,

now, as the berlios mailing lists are running again (in this context: 
sorry for the double posting of the video node-release note), i can tell 
you how to get your nodes working with getid3-1.7.5.

it's easy:

1. svn  update
2. change the "getid3"-line in your config :
    from $config['getid3dir'] = $config['basedir'] . "/code/getid3-1.6.2.";
    to $config['getid3dir'] = $config['basedir'] . 
"/code/getid3-1.7.5/getid3";

that's all. i would be happy to hear from herbert whether this solves 
bug 2a.
