hello,

getid3-1.7.5 is now implemented in the video code, also in the parts of 
editMeta.php which have recently been added from the trunk.

roland, your error was probably the correct entry in the config.inc.php 
for the new version.

for those who want already try the thing, it is
$config['getid3dir'] = $config['basedir'] . "/code/getid3-1.7.5/getid3";
and not
$config['getid3dir'] = $config['basedir'] . "/code/getid3-1.7.5";

(it has now been updated in the config.inc.template anyway.)

i will inform you as soon as the video extension is ready for "open 
testing", but there are still a few things which have to be checked 
before that milestone - especially the syncing behaviour.

à propos: we originally had planned to sync only audio content from a 
video to an audio node, but andrás opened us the eyes for the case 
A<->B<->C where A and C are video nodes and B is an audio node. In this 
case, C would never get the video programmes from node A, if A only 
synced audio programmes to B.

as we have not that much time left and the additional metadata disk 
space required is acceptable, we decided to sync all the metadata but 
modify the audio nodes in a way they do not display video programmes.

how exactly, we will work out during the next two days. so, your 
thoughts on that issue are welcome.

greetings,
martin
