Laszlo!

Hearing from you, is really nice. As I'm now also active in vienna based
Semantic Web Company (sematic-web.at), there came a lot of new ideas and
thoughts about stream-on-the-fly into my focus.

Semantic search (i know this is also a field where sztaki is interessted
in), collaborative filtering, RDF and text processing / natural language
processing are some catch-words representing techniques which may enable
SOTF to be a semantic multimedia repository.

SWC may provide expertice in bulding and maintaining thesauries, triple
stores, processing approaches and interface techniques to input into the
piece of system which SZTAKI will develop further. And the second thing is
- I really love to work with you folks.

If this is something you like to hear, let's go a step forward and discuss
on roles, workpackages and funding possibilities within the eu-framework.

Laszlo, thanks for the kick!
