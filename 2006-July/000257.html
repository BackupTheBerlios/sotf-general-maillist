Each time you run the cron.php script, it does a bunch of housekeeping in your data, including the refreshing of topic counts (which are updated immediately after any change, but accidentally they might go wrong).
So running cron.php is useful, even if you do not synchronize your content with other nodes.

Otherwise you can do the update manually on the admin screen, as Clemens has already found out.
