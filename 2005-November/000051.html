Dear StreamOnTheFly developers!

We have a request from CTV (tvprojekt.at) to organise another Community Media Sprint Event. Their development team needs again a workshop atmosphere and the StreamOnTheFly team could follow-up the progress made so far. CTV is looking forward to install a local SOTF node for managing their audio content.

Additionally, we will work on the EC funding proposals on Copyright and Web 2.0 (http://www.pvl.at/research/fp6/).

Suggested dates for the sprint event are
17-23 Nov
30 Nov - 5 Dec

Location options:
_Alberschwende, Vorarlberg (same place as at summer sprint) _Kirchbach, Styria (www.kb5.at / www.osiv.st , about 3 hours from Vienna - max 12 people (they have 6 bedrooms in the 3rd floor), plenty of meeting and desktop space for dedicated Open Source projects).
_St. Pölten ?

Please verbose your preferences or any other proposal for a meeting place until Tuesday 8 November.

Some people may have teaching or other obligations. We should try at least, to gather all relevant people at one day for a virtual meeting.
