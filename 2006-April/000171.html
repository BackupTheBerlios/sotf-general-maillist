> Hello everyone,

> how would the sync between an audio and a video node work?
> 
> My proposition is that metadata of video programmes is only synced with 
> video capable nodes, but: what is the simplest way to check in cron.php 
> whether the planned $config['video_capable']-variable of the other node

There is the sotf_nodes table. If you add a new field there named 
'video_capable', it will spread in the network using sync. It is not 
immediate, but in most cases this info will spread.
