sorry to say but in my opinion its not really good practice to create a 
certain station for a particular temporal project. such a kind the 
number of stations might increase quickly and therefore complicate 
user's orientation.

furthermore it becomes very difficult for us (orange 94.0) to explain to 
our contributors why their own series are not assigned as certain 
stations too. that's a frequently asked question, mostly argued with 
station's better visibleness. at time we have about 30 series, means 
about 30 aspriants for dedicated stations if we don't keep a more 
stringent definition.
