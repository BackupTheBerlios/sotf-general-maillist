Good point Matthias and Herbert!

1. I suggest to have a station-clean-up week, let's say 6-11 November  
2006. This should give every node admin enough time to contact people  
or move series to other stations or merge stations.

2. Any node admin can offer hosting of PodCasts. In that case, I  
suggest to either assign the PodCast to an existing station or start  
a station called "PodCaster", where we assign series for guests. Any
