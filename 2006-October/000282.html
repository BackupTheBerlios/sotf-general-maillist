both suggestions sound very well to me! i think a certain station named 
PodCaster is a good solution, even as an archive for temporal projects 
beyond the scope of a radio station/serie/broadcast.

for us (orange) as a dedicated radio project, earmarked subsidised by 
public authorities it might be problematic to argue the hosting of a 
public archive unattached to our broadcasts. since ascending interest of 
our contributors in the online archive i assume our own needs in 
filespace will increase shortly. but if necessary i could take part in 
administration, means to be a contact person (preferably one of some) 
for interested people.

true, the node was never intended to be a portal, representation should 
mainly happen elsewhere. but on the other hand rss links back to the 
archive and visitors may become interested it's content at all. that's 
why i think feasible navigation is an advantage even for the node/s.

best
herbert
