Officially, the acronym field is 30 character long, and somehow in this case 
it is felt more (maybe because of Unicode). Anyway, the acronym is usually 
shorter than the full name of the person :-). You may use the alias and name 
fields, both may contain up to 100 characters, or the intro field which can 
be very long.
