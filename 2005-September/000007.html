hi everyone,

several times i've been asked why the node of ORANGE 94.0
http://sendungsarchiv.o94.at/ is not syncronized yet. this node is a
co-production with mediawien and it's part of the agreement to transfer
the archived broadcasts of literadio and radiobande to it. we fear to
complicate this task when synchronizing before having completed the
transfer of files and metadata.

as far as i'm informed a xbmf export will be developed during the next
months. for we don't want to wait half a year janki, juergen and myself
are thinking about a quick (and probably dirty) solution. as soon as the
transfer is completed the node will certainly be synchronized with the
network.

best regards
