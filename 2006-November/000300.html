hm, for several resons i wouldn't favor this solutions. in my opinion 
this means a deep going change of content. i fear most contributors will 
have a similar point of view. at last an inbuilt jingle of a station is 
not very feasible for exchange of broadcasts with other radios (one of 
our main targets).

as recently as if you add EXTM3U and EXTINF it works correctly even with 
crappy itunes. the tags don't need to contain reasonable data, it also 
works properly with a file like this:

---snip---
#EXTM3U
#EXTINF:1,jingle
http://sendungsarchiv.o94.at/tmp/au_094st3_jingle_48kbps_1chn_22050Hz.mp3
#EXTINF:1,broadcast
http://sendungsarchiv.o94.at/tmp/au_094pr1033_parkgeschichten_128kbps_1chn_44100Hz.mp3
---snap---

in this example digit 1 represents a faked playlength (in seconds). for 
a short while this wrong values are shown by itunes, but it becomes 
recently corrected when the file starts to play. it's working properly, 
but in fact its not really unsoiled - and the value of playlength is 
already known at this time, should be no effort to merge.
